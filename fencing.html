Wood Fencing

Classic and natural-looking fencing option that offers privacy and can be customized with various stains and paints.

  • Pros: Natural appearance, customizable, good privacy
  • Cons: Requires regular maintenance, susceptible to rot and pests
  • Cost: $$ (Moderate)
  • Lifespan: 10-15 years with proper maintenance

Vinyl Fencing

Low-maintenance fencing option that resists weathering and comes in various styles and colors.

  • Pros: No painting/staining needed, durable, easy to clean
  • Cons: Higher initial cost, can become brittle in extreme cold
  • Cost: $$$ (Higher)
  • Lifespan: 20-30 years

Aluminum Fencing

Sleek and rust-resistant fencing ideal for decorative or pool enclosures, offering a modern look with minimal upkeep.

  • Pros: Rust-proof, low-maintenance, stylish appearance
  • Cons: Less privacy, may dent or bend under impact
  • Cost: $$$ (Moderate to High)
  • Lifespan: 20+ years

Chain Link Fencing

Affordable and durable fencing that offers basic security and visibility, often used in commercial or utility areas.

  • Pros: Cost-effective, durable, low maintenance
  • Cons: Offers little privacy, industrial appearance
  • Cost: $ (Low)
  • Lifespan: 15-20 years

Wrought Iron Fencing

Premium fencing option that combines strength, elegance, and timeless design. Ideal for securing properties with a touch of sophistication.

  • Pros: Extremely durable, high security, ornate appearance
  • Cons: Expensive, prone to rust if not maintained
  • Cost: $$$$ (High)
  • Lifespan: 30+ years with maintenance
